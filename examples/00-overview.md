# The Shattered Crown

## Elevator pitch
A fallen royal line hides in Hoenn while a corporation called Silph Crown harvests legendary energy.
The player is the last heir, unaware until the journey forces the truth into the open.

## Antagonist
Silph Crown — replaces Team Magma/Aqua. CEO Vance Rowe wants to weaponize Groudon and Kyogre.

## Tone
PG, serious adventure with mystery undertones.
